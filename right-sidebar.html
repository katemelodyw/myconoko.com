Our Products

Smart mushroom farming systems, premium substrates, and fungi-powered services for modern cities.

Smart Mobile Mushroom Farm

Myconoko’s Smart Mobile Mushroom Farm is a compact indoor growing system designed for urban spaces. Built for restaurants, schools, supermarkets, offices, and community environments, it brings fresh mushroom cultivation closer to where people live and eat.

The system is fully automated and designed to manage humidity, temperature, airflow, and light. It also supports cloud-based farm management and can be connected to other units for scalable deployment.

Gourmet & Medicinal Mushroom Substrates

Myconoko offers high-yield mushroom substrates for gourmet and medicinal varieties, including Lion’s Mane and Golden Oyster. These substrates are produced for freshness, reliable fruiting, and strong nutritional value.

Made from recycled wood and food waste in Hong Kong, they are produced under clean lab conditions, guaranteed to fruit within two weeks, and can yield up to 750g per bag.

Fungi-as-a-Service (FAAS)

Myconoko also provides Fungi-as-a-Service — a range of training, consultancy, education, and experiential offerings that help businesses, schools, and communities explore mushroom cultivation and sustainable urban farming.

Services include farm setup support, mushroom cultivation training, STEM and sustainability education, agri-tech consulting, and food or workshop-based experiences.
