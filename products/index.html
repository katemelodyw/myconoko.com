Smart Mobile Mushroom Farm - The Mushroom Loft

Myconoko’s Mushroom Loft is a compact indoor growing system designed for urban spaces. Built for restaurants, schools, supermarkets, offices, and community environments, it brings fresh mushroom cultivation closer to where people live and eat.

The system is fully automated and designed to manage humidity, temperature, airflow, and light. It also supports cloud-based farm management and can be connected to other units for scalable deployment.

Gourmet & Medicinal Mushroom Substrates

Myconoko offers high-yielding mushroom substrates of gourmet and medicinal varieties, including Lion’s Mane and Golden Oyster. These substrates are produced for freshness, reliable fruiting, and strong nutritional value.

Made from recycled wood and food waste in Hong Kong, they are produced under clean lab conditions, guaranteed to fruit within two weeks, and can yield up to 750g of fresh mushrooms per bag.
